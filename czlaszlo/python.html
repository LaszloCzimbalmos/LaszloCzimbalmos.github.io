Python Projects

  • Pomodoro Timer

    With just two buttons, "Start" and "Reset," you can easily manage your work sessions. When you hit "Start," the timer kicks off a 25-minute work interval, known as a pomodoro. Every other minute, I'll even throw in a motivational quote to keep you inspired! After each pomodoro, you'll get a short 5-minute break to relax and recharge. And if you want to start fresh, simply hit "Reset" to begin a new pomodoro session.

    Library: tkinter
